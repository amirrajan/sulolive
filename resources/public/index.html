Travel finance together

Track, save, plan and go.

Track you expenses together - wherever you are - and follow your progress using your own dashboards. When traveling on a budget, it's hard to account for everything when going to unfamiliar places. And maybe the route is not even fully planned yet! So play around with your numbers and find out how you can adjust your budget to fit into any new changes in your travel plans or unexpected expenses and incomes.

Be where you want to be

Multiple currencies? Limited Internet access where you hang out? We feel you. Enter your purchases in the local currency and let the app make the conversions. Keep doing the same whether you're hiking the mountains, having a drink at a hip downtown bar or camping in the jungle. Be wherever you want, and sync up when you get a connection.

Share

Are you traveling with a friend? Maybe a partner or your family? Share your dashboards with them and collaborate on tracking expenses and view your spending habits together.

Personalize

Life is unpredictable and your needs might change over time. You deserve a finance tool which is flexible enough for your life. Add your way of thinking to your finances using tags and create graphs and reports that solve your problems.

At a glance

Tags
Use tags to add context to your expenses and incomes. And use them to build detailed reports.
Currencies
Forget about conversion rates. Input your data in the local currency, and let it be converted automatically using the rate of that day.
Use Offline
No internet access? No problem, just do what you need to do offline and sync up when you get a connection.
No Passwords
No need to worry about forgetting your password, just sign in with an email link or your social media account and you're good to go.
Budgets
Do you need to separate your tracking for different use cases? Just create a new budget sheet and do your thing.
Setup goals
Create nice visualizations of your saving goals, and easier follow your progress.
Share
Share budgets and dashboards with your friends and family and collaborate on your expense tracking.
Dashboards
Create your personal dashboard for your budgets with the information that you are interested in via tags. Or just use the default graphs.
Flexible time periods
View your finances in time periods that matches your life.
