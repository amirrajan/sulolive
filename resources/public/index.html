Ask Anything

How much are you spending? Where does your money go? How long will it take you to save up for your vacation?

We don't know what questions you have and that should not hold you back. Add your own metadata to your expenses and use that to create your very own graphs and reports over the data that you're interested in.

Share

Are you traveling with a friend? Do you have a roommate or living with your partner? Share your dashboards with them and collaborate on tracking expenses and visualize your habits.

Sync across devices

Add your expenses with your mobile device when you're out and about and play around with your dashboards when you're comfortable. If you happen to be running through a no connection zone, just use the app offline and sync up when you get online.

At a glance

Tags

Use hashtags to add context to your expenses and incomes. And use them to build detailed reports.

Currencies

Forget about conversion rates. Input your data in the local currency, and let it be converted automatically using the rate of that day.

Use Offline

No internet access? No problem, just do what you need to do offline and sync up when you get a connection.

No Passwords

No need to worry about forgetting your password, just sign in with an email link or your social media account and you're good to go.

Budgets

Do you need to separate your tracking for different use cases? Just create a new budget sheet and do your thing.

Setup goals

Create nice visualizations of your saving goals, and easier follow your progress.

Share

Share budgets and dashboards and collaborate with your friends and family on your expense tracking.

Dashboards

Create your personal dashboard for your budgets with the information that you are interested in via tags. Or just use the default graphs.
